Bullet OS is created by Shrikant Bhosale, a passionate AI developer from India, with mentorship from Hintson.com.

Our mission is simple: Make AI education accessible to everyone, especially students in colleges and universities that don't have expensive GPU infrastructure.

üéØ Why Bullet OS?

The Problem: Most AI courses require expensive GPUs or cloud credits ($500-1000 per student). This creates a barrier for students in developing countries and under-resourced institutions.

Our Solution: Bullet OS runs on any CPU - even old computers! Students can train real Transformer models, learn quantization, and deploy production-ready AI - all for $0.

The Impact: Turn any computer lab into an AI research center. No barriers, no costs, just learning and innovation.
